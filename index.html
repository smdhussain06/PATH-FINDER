The Four Pillars of Ikigai

Ikigai (Áîü„ÅçÁî≤Êñê) is a Japanese concept meaning "a reason for being". It represents the intersection of four fundamental aspects of life.

What You LOVE

Your passions, interests, and what brings you joy. This represents the emotional core of your being.

What the World NEEDS

The problems you can solve and the value you can bring to society and others.

What You're GOOD AT

Your skills, talents, and natural abilities that you've developed over time.

What You Can Be PAID FOR

The economic opportunities and ways to monetize your skills and passions.

Your Ikigai lies at the intersection of these four elements.
